Introduction

Servosizer (“the App”) is a mobile application designed to assist users with servo sizing calculations. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

No Data Collected

Servosizer does not collect, store, process, share, or transmit any personal data or user information. Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Name, email, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Payment information
  • Files, photos, or media
  • Contacts or calendars
  • Any form of personal or sensitive information

Local Processing Only

All information entered into the App (such as calculation inputs) is processed locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted anywhere and cannot be accessed by the developer.

Third-Party Services

Servosizer does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, or cloud data services. The App functions entirely offline.

Children’s Privacy

Because Servosizer collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages, including children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

If Servosizer is updated in the future to collect or process data, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. Any significant changes will be posted on this page.
